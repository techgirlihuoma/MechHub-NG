The Growth Loop

01 /

Learn Theory
Understand the why — physics, maths, and concepts that make the world predictable
02 /
🔌

03 /
⚡
Get Excited
The motor spins. The sensor reads. That dopamine hit is real — use it as fuel
04 /
🔍

06 /
💥

↻ /
🚀
Repeat
Same loop, harder problems, sharper instincts. You are now one level up.
